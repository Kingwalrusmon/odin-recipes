Pizza

Description

The most amazing pizza ever made.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Pepperonni
  • Olive Oil...I think

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to about 450 or so ought to do it.
  2. Make the pizza. Lay out the pizza dough. Dump a whole bunch of pizza sauce in that big ol' circle.
  3. Bake that sucker for like 20 minutes. If it's not done check it every 5 minutes.
