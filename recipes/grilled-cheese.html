Grilled Cheese

Description

Deliciously gooey grilled cheese that will melt in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Butter the pan.
  2. Make a cheese sandwich and throw it on a medium heat buttered pan.
  3. Halfway through cooking, flip the sandwich to crisp the other side.
  4. Enjoy!
