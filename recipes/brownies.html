Brownies

Description

These delicious brownies are perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Make the brownies: Set the oven to 350 degrees and mix together the ingredients.
  2. Bake the brownies: Pour the brownie batter into a baking dish and bake 33 to 36 minutes.
  3. Serve and store: Let the brownies cool for at least 1 hour before slicing and serving.
